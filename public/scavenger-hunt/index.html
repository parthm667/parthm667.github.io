The legend of Bubbles 🐠

Our class mascot is a goldfish named Bubbles. We did not buy Bubbles. Nobody bought Bubbles. Bubbles arrived the way all legends arrive: he won himself in a suspiciously intense game of Go Fish and simply never left.

Bubbles has a problem, and the problem is gambling. He bets fish flakes on spelling tests. He runs odds on whether the fire drill is real. He once bet the classroom globe and lost it, which is why nobody in this class knows where anything is anymore.

The great flake heist

Last month, Bubbles hit the jackpot: forty-two containers of premium fish flakes on a single hand of crazy eights. But a goldfish cannot exactly open a savings account. "A bank," Bubbles says, "is just a tank with fees."
